## Anchor Alarm
**Reliable monitoring for your peace of mind at sea or on the trail.**

Anchor Alarm is a dedicated tool designed to keep you and your vessel safe. Whether you are at anchor overnight or exploring deep woods, this app monitors your position relative to a fixed point and alerts you the moment you drift beyond your set safety zone.

### Key Benefits
* **Precision Monitoring:** Tracks the exact distance and direction to your anchor point.
* **Customizable Safety Radius:** Easily adjust your "swing circle" based on the amount of chain deployed or terrain conditions.
* **Active Alerts:** Visual and haptic feedback ensures you are notified immediately if you exceed your set threshold.
* **Versatile Use:** Perfect for sailors (anchor watch) and hikers (basecamp tracker).

---

### How to Use

#### 1. Setting the Anchor
Stand as close to the anchor drop point (or campsite) as possible and press the **UP button**. The app will record these coordinates as your center point. **Hold** to reset coordinates.

#### 2. Configuring the Radius
Once the anchor is set, use the **DOWN button** to define your safety radius (e.g., 3-5 times the length of the boat). 
* **Short press:** -10m
* **Long press:** +10m

#### 3. Monitoring
While active, the watch displays your current **Distance** from the anchor and a **Blue Arrow** indicating the direction to the anchor point. You can adjust the radius on the fly if you decide to pay out more chain.

#### 4. Alarm Handling
If your boat drifts outside the circle, the "Confirm Alarm" screen will appear. The alert will persist until you manually acknowledge it, ensuring you don't miss a critical notification.


### Outdoor Lifehack
Going for a hike? "Drop anchor" at your basecamp or parked car before you head out. The app will act as a directional guide, showing you exactly which way to head back and alerting you once you've gone beyond your set limit (e.g., 100m).
